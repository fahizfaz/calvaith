Industries We Serve

Calvaith operates across multiple industries by supplying raw materials, finished products, and private-label solutions tailored to business needs. Our industry coverage is designed to be broad yet intentional, allowing us to support diverse business models under a single operational framework.

Consumer Goods & FMCG

We support brands, retailers, and distributors in the fast-moving consumer goods sector by providing both white-label manufacturing and Calvaith-owned product lines.

Includes:

  • Packaged consumer goods
  • Daily-use and lifestyle products
  • Retail and bulk FMCG items

Who we serve:

  • Emerging and established brands
  • Distributors and wholesalers
  • Retail chains and e-commerce businesses

Industrial Materials & Metals

Calvaith supplies industrial-grade raw materials and semi-processed inputs that form the backbone of manufacturing, construction, and infrastructure projects.

Includes:

  • Steel / aluminum (raw, processed, or fabricated forms)
  • Industrial materials and components
  • Custom-specification materials

Who we serve:

  • Manufacturers
  • Fabricators
  • Construction and infrastructure companies

Sports, Fitness & Recreational Equipment

We deliver finished sports and recreational products for institutional, commercial, and private branding use.

Includes:

  • Footballs and sports equipment
  • Training and recreational items
  • Custom-branded sports products

Who we serve:

  • Sports academies and clubs
  • Schools and institutions
  • Private-label sports brands

Construction, Infrastructure & Outdoor Solutions

Calvaith provides products and systems used in residential, commercial, and public infrastructure environments.

Includes:

  • Pergolas and outdoor structures
  • Modular and fabricated components
  • Project-based construction products

Who we serve:

  • Builders and developers
  • Architects and contractors
  • Commercial property owners

Custom & Multi-Industry Business Solutions

Beyond defined sectors, Calvaith supports businesses that require cross-industry sourcing, custom manufacturing, or non-standard product solutions.

This allows us to serve:

  • Startups with unique product concepts
  • Enterprises with diversified procurement needs
  • Businesses entering new product categories
