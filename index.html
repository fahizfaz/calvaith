What We Do

Calvaith operates across the entire product and supply value chain, enabling businesses to source, create, and scale products efficiently.

Raw Material Sourcing & Supply

We source and supply raw materials across multiple industries, ensuring: Quality compliance ,Volume scalability, Cost efficiency, Reliable logistics This includes industrial materials, metals, and other foundational inputs.

Finished Product Manufacturing & Supply

We provide ready-to-market products across different categories, either as standardized products, or as custom-developed solutions based on client requirements This applies to FMCG, sports goods, construction products, and more.

Private Label & OEM Solutions

Calvaith enables businesses to launch and scale products under their own brand name. Services include Product development, White-label manufacturing, Packaging and branding integration, Scalable production support. This function operates across all industries we serve.

Proprietary Product Development

We design, develop, and distribute Calvaith-owned product lines that are Unique, Market-driven, Category-specific or cross-category These products reflect Calvaith's independent innovation capability. Some of our own products are Bamboo salt,Ash,Coffe-Ashwanghanda protein bar etc

Custom & End-to-End Business Enablement

For select partners, we act as a full-cycle product and supply partner, supporting Concept to production, Sourcing to delivery ,Multi-category procurement under one entity
